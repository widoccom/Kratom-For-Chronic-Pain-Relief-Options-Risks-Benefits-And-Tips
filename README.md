**Is kratom legal in my area?**

**Can I use kratom with my current pain medications?**
Kratom can interact dangerously with many medications, especially other opioids, benzodiazepines, and certain antidepressants. Always consult with a healthcare provider before combining kratom with any other medications.

**What's the difference between kratom powder and extracts?**
Kratom powder is made from ground leaves and provides a full spectrum of alkaloids. Extracts are concentrated forms that can be significantly more potent and carry higher risks of side effects and dependence.

**How can I avoid developing tolerance to kratom?**
To minimize tolerance development, use the lowest effective dose, avoid daily use, take regular breaks from kratom, and consider rotating between different strains.

**What should I do if I experience side effects from kratom?**
If you experience mild side effects, reduce your dose or discontinue use temporarily. For severe side effects like difficulty breathing, chest pain, or severe nausea, seek immediate medical attention.

**Is kratom safer than prescription opioids for chronic pain?**
While some evidence suggests kratom may have a lower risk profile than traditional opioids, it's not risk-free. The lack of comprehensive clinical trials means the full safety profile isn't completely understood. Individual responses can vary significantly.
